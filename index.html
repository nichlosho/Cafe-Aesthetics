Learn Now! What we offer

Amazing People — Amazing Coffee

Cafe Aesthetics focuses on teaching you how to making the best coffee and latte art. We want you to be confident in your skills to ensure that you are the best in the business!

Customized Training

We want to make your learning experience to be as how you would expect. Our classes can teach you from the basics up to customized latte arts and techniques. We also offer one to one Training with our latte experts.

Cafe Interior Designs

With our international interior designers, we want to share our vision to create your very own cafe. We teach you on the basics of coffee shop aesthetics because we believe that an attrative cafe will attract more customers.

Local CLasses

We offer local classes with flexible times so that you will have a chance to attend our classes. Best practices also comes from understanding of your local communities!

Affordable

We make sure that our classes are affordable while provide modernized coffee techniques. Come and join us! We'll teach you everything you need to know about coffee making!
